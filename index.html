Our Story

Bringing Sacred Art to Your Home Since 2010

DivineMurtiStore was founded with a mission to preserve and promote the rich tradition of Hindu sacred art while making it accessible to devotees worldwide. We work directly with skilled artisans from across India who have inherited generations of craftsmanship in creating divine murtis and pooja items.

Every piece in our collection is handcrafted with devotion and blessed in traditional ceremonies before being shipped to your home. We believe that these sacred items are not just decorative pieces but channels of divine energy that enhance your spiritual practice.

Authentic Craftsmanship

Each item is handcrafted by skilled artisans using traditional techniques.

Pan-India Delivery

We deliver to all states in India with secure packaging for fragile items.

Eco-Friendly

We use sustainable materials and eco-friendly packaging.
